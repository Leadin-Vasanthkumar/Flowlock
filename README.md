# Flowlock


> **Don't just track your time. Lock in your flow.**


Flowlock is a high-performance productivity ecosystem designed to help you reclaim your focus and structure your life. It combines deep focus task management with guided biological recovery to ensure sustained peak performance without the burnout.

---

## 💡 The Why: Beyond the Random To-Do List

I built Flowlock because I was tired of living a "random" life. 

Traditional to-do lists felt like endless buckets of demands that didn't respect my focus or my time. I felt like I couldn't track my progress effectively, and I wanted a **structured life** where my day was intentional, not accidental.

The goal was simple: 
1. **Conserve Focus**: Stop leaking mental energy on deciding what to do next.
2. **Integrated Structure**: Merging a to-do list with a structured daily timeline.
3. **Biological Balance**: Understanding that focus requires recovery.

Flowlock isn't just an app; it's the structure I needed to take back control of my day.

---

## 🚀 Outstanding Features

What makes Flowlock different from every other productivity app?

### 1. The Focus-Recovery Loop
Most apps only care about how much you "work." Flowlock cares about how you recover. When you finish a deep work session, you don't just stop—you are guided through **Biological Resets**:
*   **Box Breathing**: Instant nervous system regulation.
*   **NSDR (Non-Sleep Deep Rest)**: Deep mental recharging.
*   **Physical Resets**: Guided stretches and walking resets.
*   **Creative Doodling**: Mental "defocusing" to foster insight.

### 2. Structured Daily Timeline
Move away from "maybe" and "later." Flowlock organizes your tasks into a visual timeline, helping you visualize your day's flow from 1 AM to 11 PM.

### 3. High-Performance Analytics
Track your **Consistency Grid** and focus metrics. Don't just see what you did—see how consistently you're showing up for yourself.

### 4. Premium Aesthetic & Experience
A dark-mode, glassmorphic design built for the modern professional. Featuring interactive background orbs and smooth Framer Motion animations that make productivity feel premium.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

Flowlock is built with a modern, high-performance stack:

*   **Frontend**: React 19 (Latest) & TypeScript
*   **Build Tool**: Vite
*   **Styling**: Tailwind CSS 4 & Framer Motion for high-end animations
*   **Backend & Auth**: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
*   **Visuals**: Lucide Icons & Custom OGL/WebGL Graphics
*   **Charts**: Recharts for performance tracking

### 🤖 100% AI Built
One of the most unique aspects of Flowlock: **Not a single line of code was written by a human.** This entire application was architected, written, and debugged through collaboration with AI, proving that the future of software development is here.
